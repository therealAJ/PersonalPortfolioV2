Hi, it's Alex here. I'm a second year student at the University of British Columbia majoring in both Business and Computer Science (BUCS). The BUCS program has allowed me to immerse myself in both the technology and commerce worlds, forming a perfect blend of the areas I am passionate about.

I am fueled by big ideas, motivated action, and both exceptional problem-solving and communication skills developed through extensive extra-curricular and academic experiences. I believe in finding the areas in your life where you excel and improving the world by contributing in those fields. Personally, I am determined to bridge the gap between Business and Technology, using my zest for computer science to complement my entrepreneurial savvy.

Aside from academia and coding, I define myself as an athlete. Over the past 5 years, basketball has become a part of the individual I am today. Although my journey started with a summer camp, it led to me falling in love with the game and eventually to being a part of the U17 Basketball BC Provinicial Team, being named a Top 15 Senior in the province, winning the same award Steve Nash did almost 20 years ago, back-to-back Island Championships and numerous other unforgettable experiences.
